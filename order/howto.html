How to Order Online

Quick steps to get your sizzling favorites — dine-in or take-out.

Step-by-Step

  1. Go to stonegrillresto.net/order (mobile or desktop).
  2. Choose a category (Pork, Chicken, Seafood, etc.), then tap Add beside each dish.
  3. Open Your Order to review and adjust quantities or remove items.
  4. Fill in your details:
    • Full Name & Mobile Number (auto-formats to +63)
    • Order Type: Dine-in or Take-out
      • If Dine-in, enter Number of Persons.
    • Date & Time for dine-in/collection
    • Special Requests (optional)
  5. Click Submit Order. You’ll see a confirmation toast.
  6. Our team will message you via SMS/Messenger with payment instructions.
  7. Pay via GCash or in-store. We process orders once payment is confirmed.

Tips for a Smooth Order

  • Double-check your Date & Time before submitting.
  • Keep your phone on — we may contact you for clarifications.
  • Have your GCash ready for quick confirmation.
