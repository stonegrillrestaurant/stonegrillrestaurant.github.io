About Us

Welcome to Stone Grill Restaurant – your go-to destination for a unique dining experience.

Founded in 2014, we introduced the concept of hot stone cooking to our community, allowing guests to enjoy freshly grilled meals right at their table. This interactive style of dining makes every visit memorable and perfect for families, friends, and special celebrations.

We pride ourselves on using only high-quality ingredients, hand-picked by our culinary team. Our specialties include seafood, sizzling plates, Filipino favorites, and grilled meats, each served with warmth and a smile.

Our Mission

To deliver delicious food and create a relaxing atmosphere where every guest feels right at home. We believe that good food, good service, and a good ambiance go hand in hand in making unforgettable moments.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Cook your meal your way with our hot stone grilling method
  • ✅ Friendly and attentive service
  • ✅ Family-friendly environment
  • ✅ Centrally located at the heart of Bato, Leyte
