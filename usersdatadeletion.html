Facebook User Data Deletion Instructions

If you signed in to our site using Facebook Login and wish to delete the data associated with your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Go to your Facebook Account Settings.
  2. Click on Apps and Websites.
  3. Find and select Stone Grill Restaurant.
  4. Click Remove to delete the app and associated permissions.

This will delete the connection between your Facebook account and our website. We do not store your data on our servers. If you wish to confirm deletion or have questions, contact us below.
